Chicken and Rice

Simple Everyday Meal

Ingredients

Skin on Chicken Breast, Mushrooms, Garlic, Butter, Olive Oil, Chicken Stock, Rice


Directions

Saute mushrooms in butter and oil. Add chicken skin side down and brown outside. Add Garlic, Rice, Stock. Cover and Simmer for 40 Min.





Sliders

Best Burgers

Ingredients

Ground Chuck (80% lean /20% fat), Yellow American Cheese, Potato Rolls


Directions

Gently form meat into loose patties approximately the size of a 1/2 cup measuring cup. Preheat nonstick pan to high. Add patties and flip once half way. Add cheese slice and serve on potato rolls.
